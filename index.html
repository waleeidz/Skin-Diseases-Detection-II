Skin Condition Information

Learn about common skin conditions with easy-to-understand information

Acne

A common skin condition that occurs when hair follicles become clogged with oil and dead skin cells, leading to pimples, blackheads, or whiteheads.

Common Causes: Hormonal changes, excess oil production, bacteria, certain medications
Commonly Affected: Face, forehead, chest, upper back, shoulders

Eczema

A condition that causes skin to become itchy, red, dry, and cracked. It's also known as atopic dermatitis and is common in children but can occur at any age.

Common Causes: Genetics, immune system dysfunction, environmental triggers, allergens
Commonly Affected: Inner elbows, behind knees, hands, face, scalp

Vitiligo

A condition where the skin loses its pigment cells (melanocytes), resulting in discolored patches. It can affect any area of the body and occurs in all skin types.

Common Causes: Autoimmune condition, genetic factors, oxidative stress
Commonly Affected: Face, hands, arms, feet, areas around body openings

These descriptions are for educational purposes only. Skin conditions can vary greatly between individuals. Please consult a dermatologist for proper diagnosis and treatment.
